About Factum Humanum

Certifying Authenticity in a Digital Age

Our Mission

Factum Humanum .org exists to give creators a way to register their work and advertise and celebrate the fact that it is human created.

What We Do

We offer a registry, free to use, which is groomed and pruned if neccesary, periodically. Alongside this, people can use the logo on their work.

Easy Registration

Registration involves filling a form. No spam is generated as a result of registering.

Certification and Logo

Receive a registration certificate and use our logo to advertise your human-created work.
